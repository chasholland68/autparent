STEP is a local Nashville nonprofit that helped me personally to figure out my kid's public school options when we first moved to Nashville. It stands for Support and Training for Exceptional Parents, and they offer "Special Education Assistance for Tennessee Families." I was knew to the Autism world while also being knew to the area, and they were able to explain things to me in a way that actually made sense in a very overwhelming time. Do you know your child's rights when it comes to their education? STEP can help. FIVE MF'in STARS!
